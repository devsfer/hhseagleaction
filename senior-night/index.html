Episode Summary

In this episode, we interview a student cheerleader, football player, and Coach Williams (SV Football Coach). They each give us a perspective of what it's like in different activities at the Huntsville High School. Senior Night just happened, we won! So we're recapping some of those moments in this episode.

Our cross country just went to Hot Springs to compete at State (PS. We won), so we're wishing them luck.

Final reminder that the last date to register for the ACT December 11th date is today, Friday, Nov. 5, so go register. We're also going next week to playoffs (woo!), so that's being hosted in Ozark, please go to support our Eagles!

About The Eagle Action Podcast

    The Eagle Action Podcast is a weekly podcast for Huntsville High School students to learn about sports schedules, national test dates and news from around the building. Tune in to hear from some of your fellow classmates, teachers, and special guest speakers. New episodes released every Friday.
