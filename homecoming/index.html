Episode Summary

This week is homecoming week! (Yes, we're a little behind) To celebrate homecoming week, we asked three different seniors questions about their favorite experience this week and also got some updates on this week's sports schedule.

The ACT test date is approaching, so we're reminding you to register for either the October 23rd, test date, or December 11th test date. The December 11th test date registration deadline is on November 5th. Register soon at myact.org.

About The Eagle Action Podcast

    The Eagle Action Podcast is a weekly podcast for Huntsville High School students to learn about sports schedules, national test dates and news from around the building. Tune in to hear from some of your fellow classmates, teachers, and special guest speakers. New episodes released every Friday.
