Episode Summary

Intro to the HHS Eagle Action Podcast by the Huntsville High School Drumline.

Show Notes:

This trailer includes the intro of the HHS Eagle Action Podcast. The intro feature the Huntsville High School Drumline playing Steel Feel. Drummers heard include:

  • Daniel Sandoval - Snare 1 - Section leader
  • Hunter McNeer - Snare 2
  • Hunter Thomas - Tenor Drums
  • Angel Torres - Bass 1
  • Clayton Henry - Bass 2
  • Kelvin Orduna - Bass 3
  • Riley Hellyer - Bass 4

This episode was directed by Daniel Sandoval and Produced by Kelvin Orduna.

About The Eagle Action Podcast

    The Eagle Action Podcast is a weekly podcast for Huntsville High School students to learn about sports schedules, national test dates and news from around the building. Tune in to hear from some of your fellow classmates, teachers, and special guest speakers. New episodes released every Friday.
