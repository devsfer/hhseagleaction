Episode Summary

In this episode, we introduce the Eagle Action Podcast and announce this week's (Sept. 27 - Oct. 1) sports. In this episode, we also included a special 'Senior Spotlight' section with four questions. We also have a special guest, Mr. Sampson, our newest English teacher to tell us how his transition from Michigan was to Northwest Arkansas. In this episode, we also briefly discussed next week's Homecoming schedule and themes.

About Eagle Action

    The Eagle Action Podcast is a weekly podcast for Huntsville High School students to learn about sports schedules, national test dates and news from around the building. Tune in to hear from some of your fellow classmates, teachers, and special guest speakers. New episodes released every Friday.
