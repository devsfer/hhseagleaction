Episode Summary

Baseball! This week's guest speaker is Coach Harris who talks to us about his baseball team as well as his thoughts on it. Why does Coach Harris not like graduations? Learn why on this episode.

This week's senior spotlights include Tucker Bradley and Braden Carter discussing their 2022 Baseball season. Savannah Villines also gives us information about Huntsville High School's 2022 Prom.

    The Eagle Action Podcast is a weekly podcast for Huntsville High School students to learn about sports schedules, national test dates and news from around the building.
