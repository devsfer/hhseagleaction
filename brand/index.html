What is HHS Eagle Action?

HHS Eagle Action (Huntsville High School Eagle Action) is a is a podcast for high school students, particularly Huntsville High School students to hear advice from their teachers, fellow students, and guest speakers from throughout Arkansas. 

The HHS Eagle Action Podcast is based in the Huntsville High School in Huntsville, Arkansas. The podcast is not owned by the school, but student lead with moderation from the Director of Theatre and Arts, Wendy Wiggins, and supervision from the Principal of Huntsville High School, Roxanne Enix.

1. Logo

This is our primary logo, note the black text and eagle. 

When the primary logo doesn't fit your composition, use the single eagle icon.

1.1 Clearspace

Our logo should always have space to breathe.

Don't put stuff within the maroon borders, we claim that space.

1.2 Color

On light backgrounds, use our black logo

On dark backgrounds, use our white logo.

1.3 Logo Don'ts

Don't tilt or rotate our logo.

Don't change the color of our logo from our approved designs. 

Don't use a light background with a light version of our logo. Same with a dark background with a dark version of our logo.

About The Eagle Action Podcast

    The Eagle Action Podcast is a weekly podcast for Huntsville High School students to learn about sports schedules, national test dates and news from around the building. Tune in to hear from some of your fellow classmates, teachers, and special guest speakers. New episodes released every Friday.
