Episode 6: Florida, Choir, and Things You Should Know

Episode 6 • Summary • Show Notes

Listen on Apple Podcasts

Episode Summary

This week we welcome back the baseball team from their Spring Break trip to Florida. We feature Dax Wiggins, a current sophomore, to tell us about their trip to Florida and its importance to the team's success. We also want to congratulate Kelvin Orduna for placing first in Website Design and Kelli Meythaler for placing 3rd in Cybersecurity at the Arkansas FBLA State Leadership Conference. This week, the Huntsville High School Choir also went to State and received the highest ratings they have in over two decades. Congratulations to everyone for representing HHS and we're proud of you.

Hurry and purchase your Prom tickets! This upcoming week (April 11th-15th, 2022) is the last week we'll be offering Prom tickets. Prom will be hosted at Willow Brooke Farms and doors will open at 6 PM. You can purchase tickets outside of the cafeteria during both lunches. Single tickets are $20 and couple tickets are $30.

We interviewed Leah Daugherty, a choir student, who gave us an inside look at how they prepared for State as well as a recording of the HHS Choir singing at State (5:33).

Movie Talk: Listen in with Daniel Sandoval and Hunter McNeer with this week's Movie Talk segment over The Batman.
