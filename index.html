Hunter McNeer

Co-host

Hunter McNeer is a junior at the Huntsville High School and co-host of the Eagle Action Podcast. Hunter is a drummer for the Huntsville High School Marching Band. Hunter is involved in film and photography.
In his free time, Hunter watches movies.

Kelvin Orduna

head of marketing
Eagle Action Podcast

Kelvin Orduna is the Head of Marketing for the Eagle Action Podcast at the Huntsville High School. Kelvin has experience with digital marketing, web design, podcast production, and audio. Kelvin has worked with the Eagle Action team to bring you entertainment from Huntsville High School's very own students.

Mrs. Wendy Wiggins

director of theatre and Arts
Huntsville High School

Mrs. Wendy Wiggins is the Director of Theatre and Arts at Huntsville High School.  Mrs. Wiggins has taught theater and performance for over 25 years.  She is very excited to explore the new world of Podcasting and Digital Films.  She hopes that the program will continue to grow and be successful.
