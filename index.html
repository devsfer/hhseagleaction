Staff

Meet HHS Eagle Action's hosts and the people running the show.

Daniel Sandoval

co-host

Daniel Sandoval is a junior at the Huntsville High School and co-host of the Eagle Action Podcast. Daniel is the section leader for percussion in the Huntsville High School Marching Band and can be heard in the intro of the Eagle Action Podcast. 

Elly Harriman

host

Elly Harriman is a junior at the Huntsville High School and host of The Eagle Action Podcast. She is involved in various activities including basketball and student council. As a host of the Eagle Action Podcast, she writes scripts, interviews, and also introduces others to the podcast.

Hunter McNeer

Co-host

Hunter McNeer is a junior at the Huntsville High School and co-host of the Eagle Action Podcast. Hunter is a drummer for the Huntsville High School Marching Band. Hunter is involved in film and photography. In his free time, Hunter watches movies.

Kelvin Orduna

Producer / marketing

Kelvin Orduna is the producer for the Eagle Action Podcast at the Huntsville High School. Kelvin has experience with digital marketing, web design, podcast production, and audio. Kelvin has worked with the Eagle Action Media team to bring you entertainment from Huntsville High School's very own students.

Mrs. Wendy Wiggins

director of theatre and Arts
Huntsville High School

Mrs. Wendy Wiggins is the Director of Theatre and Arts at Huntsville High School.  Mrs. Wiggins has taught theater and performance for over 25 years.  She is very excited to explore the new world of Podcasting and Digital Films.  She hopes that the program will continue to grow and be successful. 

About The Eagle Action Podcast

    The Eagle Action Podcast is a weekly podcast for Huntsville High School students to learn about sports schedules, national test dates and news from around the building. Tune in to hear from some of your fellow classmates, teachers, and special guest speakers. New episodes released every Friday.
